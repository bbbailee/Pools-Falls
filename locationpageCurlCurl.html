Curl Curl Beach
New South Wales
50mins from Sydney

Curl Curl Beach, located on the New South Wales coast, is a stunning stretch of coastline known for its golden sands and excellent surfing conditions.

This picturesque destination, situated between Freshwater and Dee Why beaches, offers a relaxing and family-friendly atmosphere, making it a popular spot for beachgoers and outdoor enthusiasts. Curl Curl Beach is a haven for beach lovers and surfers, boasting pristine waters and ample opportunities for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports. It's a serene escape on the Northern Beaches of Sydney.

The beach is surrounded by lush coastal vegetation and scenic sand dunes, providing a natural buffer between the shoreline and nearby residential areas. Native flora and wildlife add to the coastal charm.

"Golden sands, surfing, and coastal beauty near Sydney."
Tip: Make sure you swim between the flags

Getting There
Curl Curl Beach is easily accessible by car or public transport, making it a convenient destination for day trips from Sydney and the surrounding areas. Driving
 From Sydney, take the Manly Scenic Walkway, which leads to Curl Curl Beach, or drive via Pittwater Road. There is ample parking available near the beach.

Public Transport
 Sydney's efficient public transport system includes buses that can take you to Curl Curl Beach from various parts of the city and Northern Beaches.

Tours and Guided Trips
 While there may not be specific tours for Curl Curl Beach, you can explore the beach independently or join broader Northern Beaches tours.

Camping
Camping is not available at Curl Curl Beach, as it primarily offers day-use amenities. Nearby parks and campgrounds may provide overnight accommodations.
